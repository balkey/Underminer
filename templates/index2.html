ABOUT

"We'll do the drilling for you, but you're coming down too!"

UNDERMINER is a web-application trying to understand how human minds work in written form. It is not a scientific research tool, though with time we hope to reach the academia with our findings.


In the meantime there will be new functions rollin' out frequently, but our primal goal remains simple: bring out Natural Language Processing (NLP) tools and methods for everyone who's dealing with text either proffessionally or as a closet-author.


Let's assume you've produced some text that is longer than a sketch-note on a napkin. Regular text editors might tell you how many characters / words you have bashed into the computer (some even know about paragraphs and sentences), but that's not a lot, right?


What about your vocabulary - similar words and synonymes? Are you an adjective-stacker or you rather keep things strict and objective? Do you use a lot of panels? Compared to who? What about the main emotions your writing is beaming of? We believe your unique personality hides there deep inside your text, and even hope to tell you new things about yourself!


1. UPLOAD FILE

Only .txt files
Please make sure your text is UTF-8 encoded. Otherwise the analysis will be a lot slower!
The script only understands standard English currently... behold!


2. WAIT FOR ANALYSIS

In a couple of seconds we slice and dice your text
Generate interactive charts for you
Compile a library consisting of .json and .tsv files and images


3. GET YOUR JUICES FLOWIN'

Give some clicks to the charts, move swiftly through chapters, sentences and n-grams
Download your text in structered .tsv / .json files, for further adventures
Reuse your high-resolution .png charts for your presentations, publications or posts!
