ABOUT
UNDERMINER works best with fiction in it's current form - to get a feel of what the application can do, we encourage you to upload your favorite literary text for a fun starter. We strongly recommend that you use texts downloaded from the Gutenberg Project's website, where our examples are from - these literary works are available with "almost no restriction whatsoever". Also please make sure to use the Plain-text UTF-8 encoded versions of the files. The following examples are meant to present a preface to how linear plots can be accessed through text analysis. As you'll probably notice, the engine has many flaws and bugs - fixing those is underway!
